The judge announces, "Mr.Poldi Breschfeld is accusing you of being a witch. He said he saw you perform some sort of witchcraft the other day and five of his slaves were suddenly dead. What you have to say about this statement?"

You are surprised to hear that Poldi is accusing you as a witch, yet at the same time, you aren't. You believe he is framing you as a witch is a way to take over your farm.

You say:

"I confess. I am a witch."
"I deny. I am not a witch."
