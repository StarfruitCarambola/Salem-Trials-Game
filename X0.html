After a couple hours, the two guards bring you from your cell down the hall to a large rooms. There is fireplace burns warmly in the corner along with various benches and metal tools on the side. A man with a large robe is sitting behind a table.

He speaks, "Welcome to the questioning chamber. I am Doctor Obadiah, the magistrate here to investigate the scourge of witchcraft here in Salem. You have been accused on good evidence of the crime of witchcraft. If you answer my questions, we do not need to resort to torture. If, however, you resist the truth, you will suffer through the infernal machinations of Hell. Then we must compel you to answer. Will you confess to being a witch?"

You say:

"I confess. I am a witch."
