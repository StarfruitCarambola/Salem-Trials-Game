You say you confess that you are a witch. Knowing that they won't believe you if you deny it and would suffer eternal pain, you decide to confess hoping they would believe you and let you go free.

The magistrate says, "Now that you have admitted to being a witch, let us be clear about what you have done in the name of Satan. We must have a proper, written signed confession. Answer the following questions. The scribe will record your answers. If your answers are not satisfactory, torture will be applied."
