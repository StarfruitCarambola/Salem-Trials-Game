You answered all the questions and make your mark on a document of the confession. The magistrate is pleased. The guards return you to your cell and you get a good meal for the night. The next day you are back in the courtroom. You stand alone with only the judge. You swear on the Bible that you will tell the truth.

The judge asks, "You stand accused of being a witch and practicing diabolical arts to the harm of the commonwealth. Now, before this court how do you plead: are you innocent or are you a witch?"

You confess that you are a witch.
